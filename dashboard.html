Welcome to the Dashboard of your Finance Management

Welcome to our Finance Management Dashboard, your one-stop solution for managing your personal finances efficiently and effectively. Our platform offers a range of tools designed to help you keep track of your income, expenses, and financial goals. Whether you're a student managing loans or someone looking to get a better handle on your finances, our dashboard is here to help.

Features

  • Record Entries: Easily record your financial transactions, categorize them as income or expense, and choose between cash or online transactions.
  • View Ledger: Keep a detailed and organized record of all your financial transactions in the ledger.
  • Manage Student Loans: Keep track of your student loans, including payment schedules and outstanding balances.
  • Get Help: Access a variety of resources and support to help you better manage your finances.

Getting Started

To get started, simply navigate to the Record Entries tab to begin inputting your financial transactions. Use the Ledger tab to review all your entries and keep track of your financial health. If you need any assistance, don't hesitate to visit the Help tab.
